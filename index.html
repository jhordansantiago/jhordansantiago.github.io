I recently graduated in Physics from the Federal University of Campina Grande (UFCG), advised by Prof. Danieverton Moretti. My research focused on theoretical quantum optics, particularly on photon statistics, the phase-space formulation of quantum mechanics, and photon-added and photon-subtracted states. I am also interested in the foundations of science, especially its axiomatic and philosophical aspects.

My undergraduate research was supported by a federal grant.

Outside of research, I enjoy running (mostly half-marathons) and spending time at cafés. Lacrimosa is my favorite band, and my favorite film is Béla Tarr’s Sátántangó (1991), based on the novel of the same name by László Krasznahorkai.
